About Us

Chef Amandeep made this restaurant purely from his passion for cooking meals for people. The restaurant opened in 2020. The name Aman's Anabolic Kitchen was created over a meal of Thai Country Noodles. Chef Amandeep loves eating food and loves working out. Although the menu isn't that healthy, chef Amandeep does say to have a nice meal once in a while during your cutting season so you can keep losing weight. The restaurant is available in 3 places, check out the locations section to find out where the restaurants are available. You can also call the number or email the contact provided in contact us section for any inquires about the restaurant. Please call us in order to reserve a seat in the diner. The call number is found in the contact us section of this page. Below is the menu, it also states what is in each dish.Please tell the chef about any alergies before you place your order.

Ingredients Used

Chicken Ramen Noodles
Ramen noodles in chicken broth with steamed vegetbales, along with hard-boiled egg, and garnished with green onions
Sushi
Salmon and Tuna Sushi are made of rolls of vinegar-flavored cold cooked rice containing a garnish of raw fish of your choice and select vegetbales.
California Roll is a small roll of vinegar-flavored cold cooked rice containing cucumber, crab (imitation crab may be used at upon diner's request), and avocado.
Tea
Tea is a beverage made from cured/fresh leaves. It is commonly prepared with hot or boiling water.
Ice Cream
Ice cream is a soft frozen dessert made with flavored milk and sweeteners, we have 3 flavors to choose from.Our Ice Cream contains milk and gluten.
